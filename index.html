HOW IT WORKS ?

As simple as a chatting app.

VIA PHONE NUMBER

You send tokens to a number from your contacts, the receiver gets a
message along with a OTP that he has to enter to claim the tokens. All
of this happens by simply chatting with our Pro-bot, that assists you
through out the process along with suggestions.

VIA LINK

You deposit tokens and share the link, then the receiver will get the
simple instructions how to easily create wallet and claim tokens
